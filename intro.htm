DO IT FASTER, SMARTER, BETTER.

TrekSoft's SAUT is a highly interactive Survey Authoring Tool powerful enough to handle professional research questionnaires yet simple and fun to use as a casual form generator. Offering a wide choice of interactive interface objects aside the usual check boxes, radio buttons, text fields, lists and such, it lets you widen the application fields and go beyond the "run of the mill" surveys. With Treksoft's SAUT you are able to integrate to your questionnaires not only multimedia objects such as images, animations, video and audio, but also interactive diagrams, puzzles, charts, maps, and more. The geolocation object* based on Google maps, Yahoo maps, MS maps and other mapping service providers lets you take your surveys to a whole new level. Learn more about SAUT's features from the Help menu and from Treksoft's website.
